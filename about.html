About me

Salesforce delivery at the intersection of people and process

I work where project leadership, operations and hands-on delivery meet — turning complex Salesforce Marketing Cloud and technology projects into clear, on-time outcomes without losing sight of the people doing the work or the customers it's for.

I work at the intersection of project leadership, operations and delivery. My background combines hands-on execution with leadership experience across Salesforce Marketing Cloud, agile ways of working, and process improvement — and what sets me apart is a psychology background that strengthens team dynamics and customer engagement.

As an Application Consultant and Technical Project Manager at VML MAP (formerly Wunderman Thompson MAP), I deliver and maintain Salesforce Marketing Cloud for a worldwide brand — from builds and automations to a full platform migration off Adobe Campaign — while facilitating Scrum ceremonies, creating uniform procedures, and keeping delivery on track during critical business peaks.

I hold a deep Salesforce certification stack — Marketing Cloud Consultant, Data Cloud Consultant, AI Specialist and more — alongside Professional Scrum Master I & II and PMI-ACP. My academic foundation is in psychology (BSc with honours and an MSc), which gives me an edge in understanding motivation, behaviour and communication, so I can bridge the technical, the commercial and the human sides of a project.

I'm based in Copenhagen and always eager to keep growing in martech, agile delivery and the fast-evolving field of Salesforce.
