About me

People-first project management, backed by data

I help teams turn complex digital and technology projects into clear, on-time delivery — without losing sight of the people doing the work or the customers it's for.

I'm an accomplished project manager with over four years of success in the digital marketing and technology sectors. What sets me apart is a unique psychological background that enhances team dynamics and customer engagement — I lead with data-driven strategies, but I never forget that projects are delivered by people.

Across my roles I've focused on improving project efficiency and performance: building comprehensive plans, managing delivery lifecycles with Agile methodologies, and keeping stakeholders aligned from kickoff to launch. I'm known for strong leadership, clear communication, and a consistent track record of delivering on time and within budget.

My academic foundation is in psychology — bachelor's and master's degrees — which gives me an edge in understanding motivation, behaviour and communication. I pair that with formal project management training and certifications, so I can bridge the technical, the commercial and the human sides of a project.

I'm based in Copenhagen and always eager to keep growing in the fast-evolving field of project management.
